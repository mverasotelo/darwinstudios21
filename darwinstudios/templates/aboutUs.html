About Us

Darwin Studios is a complex of locations, events room, office space and private studios that arises from the reconversion of one of the oldest furniture factories in the City of Buenos Aires.

The original remodeling preserved the industrial spirit of the more than 7,000 m2 that the building has, located in the Audiovisual District of Palermo.

The complex offers a variety of spaces and facilities that make it a unique place.

Warehouses with 9-meter-high ceilings and technical walkways with grills that favor assembly, factory-type windows, industrial facades, a spacious exterior that includes a cobblestone street, terraces, ramp to enter vehicles, professional kitchen, offices, warehouses, old stairs, forklift and engine room.

We work every day with the aim of facilitating access to our House for all content creators.
